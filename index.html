Whitney Little | Graphic Design Professional

www.whitneylittle.net

A native to the Greater Seattle area, Whitney holds a diverse background in Graphic Design.

Her expertise includes branding, web design, advertisements, editorial layouts, marketing collateral and illustration. Currently working as an Associate Art Director, Whitney designs for a monthly readership of over two million people. With a steady stream of side projects, she also spends her time designing things such as wedding invitations, site wireframes and magazine advertisements. When not at her desk, Whitney has a passion for the arts. She studies dance, adores photography and loves to support local performers as often as possible.
